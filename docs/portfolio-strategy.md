# Portfolio Strategy

This portfolio is designed to show a professional transition from analytics foundations into data engineering, data platforms, ML engineering, and AI engineering.

## Narrative

The portfolio starts with practical analytics work: SQL exploration, data cleaning, visualization, and business analysis. It then moves into the Lexian Engineering Portfolio, a fictional fintech engineering ecosystem focused on transaction processing, reconciliation, risk, fraud, data platforms, ML systems, and AI systems.

## Principles

- Be honest about project status.
- Mark future work as Planned.
- Keep each major Lexian product in its own repository.
- Use synthetic data only.
- Explain business context and engineering tradeoffs.
- Prefer small, testable systems over large unfinished scaffolds.

## Review Experience

A recruiter or data lead should be able to understand the portfolio quickly:

1. Profile README explains who I am and where I am going.
2. Portfolio Hub organizes the work.
3. Lexian Transaction Engine demonstrates active engineering practice.
4. Foundation projects show the analytics base.
5. Planned projects show the growth path without overstating completion.
